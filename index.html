Text to Speech .

With the text-to-speech feature, you can effortlessly understand what the deaf want to express by typing any words in Tagalog or English. This helps reduce communication barriers between the deaf and hearing individuals, making communication easier and more inclusive."

Speech to SL & Quiz Game .

"With the speech-to-sign language feature, hearing individuals can convey any message they want by using the microphone on their device, which then translates the message into sign language for the deaf to understand, without requiring any hand gestures. Additionally, our quiz game allows users to improve their sign language skills by learning words and letters through the dictionary content, making it an engaging and educational tool for learning sign language."
